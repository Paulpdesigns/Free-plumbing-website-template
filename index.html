How the range is built

Every estimate starts from the same rate sheet, then adjusts for scope and timing the same way it would over the phone.

  • 1 A base rate for the service you picked, drawn from the last twelve months of completed jobs.
  • 2 A scope multiplier for job size, since a single fixture and a whole property are not priced the same way.
  • 3 A commercial adjustment where it applies, covering the extra coordination that business sites usually need.
  • 4 A same day surcharge only if you ask for emergency timing instead of standard scheduling.
